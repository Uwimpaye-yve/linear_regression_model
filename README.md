## Mission Description
Mission: To empower vulnerable children by ensuring access to education and healthcare, creating a foundation for long-term mental well-being and resilience.
Problem: Public health officials need to understand how schooling and childhood immunizations (Hepatitis B, Polio, Diphtheria) directly predict the long-term life expectancy of children in developing nations.


## Data Source
** Dataset: ** WHO Life Expectancy Data (sourced from Kaggle).
